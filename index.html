Australian Health Insurance Claiming Process

General Insurance Claim Process

Claiming health insurance benefits in Australia typically involves understanding what your policy covers, receiving healthcare services, and submitting a claim to be reimbursed or to have costs covered directly by your insurer.

  • Review Coverage: Familiarize yourself with your policy benefits and what services are eligible.
  • Receive Care: Obtain medical treatment or allied health services from providers.
  • Submit Claim: Claims can be lodged online via insurer portals/apps, through direct billing by healthcare providers, by mail with receipts, or in-app using photo receipt capture.
  • Assessment: Your insurer reviews the claim for validity and coverage.
  • Payment: Approved claims are reimbursed, usually to your nominated bank account, or paid directly to the provider.
  • Follow Up: Respond promptly to any insurer requests for additional information to avoid delays.

Processes vary slightly between insurers and plans, so always check specific policies or contact customer support if unsure.

Bupa

Claiming Options

  • Online Portal & App: Submit claims quickly and easily by uploading receipts.
  • Direct Billing: Many Bupa-approved providers bill Bupa directly, avoiding out-of-pocket expenses.
  • In-App Photo Claims: Snap and send receipt photos via the Bupa app for fast processing.

Claims are generally processed within 1-3 business days. Make sure receipts detail provider info, dates, and services clearly for smooth processing.

More info: Bupa How to Claim

Medibank

How to Claim

  • Online via Medibank Member Portal or App: The fastest claim lodgment option.
  • Automatic Claims: Some providers directly submit claims on your behalf to Medibank.
  • Claim by Post: Submit claim forms and receipts by mail if preferred.

Claims processing generally takes 1-5 business days with payments made to bank accounts or credited to premiums.

Details: Medibank Claims

Allianz

Submitting Claims

  • MyHealth Portal & App: Submit and track claims easily online.
  • Direct Billing: Select providers may bill Allianz directly, reducing upfront costs.
  • Manual Submission: Mail claim forms and receipts if preferred.

Ensure receipts itemize all services. Online claims typically process within 2-4 business days.

Learn more: Allianz How to Claim

NAB Health Insurance (via partners)

NAB health insurance is provided through partnerships with insurers. Claims procedures depend on your specific insurance provider but generally follow similar patterns:

  • Use the insurer’s online portal or app for claims.
  • Direct billing may be available for certain providers.
  • Claims can often be submitted with receipts via mail or electronically.

Refer to your NAB policy or contact NAB for exact details.

More info: NAB Health Insurance

Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC)

OSHC policies help international students in Australia manage medical expenses. The claims process is similar but with particular emphasis on certain providers and services.

How to Claim OSHC

  • Use your OSHC provider’s app or online portal to submit claims.
  • Check if your medical or hospital provider offers direct billing to your OSHC insurer to minimize out-of-pocket costs.
  • Submit claims with original or digital receipts if direct billing is not available.
  • Contact your OSHC provider’s customer service if uncertain about coverage or claim procedures.

Popular OSHC providers include:
Medibank OSHC, Bupa OSHC, Allianz Care Australia OSHC, and others.

Always carry your OSHC membership card and claim documents for faster processing.
